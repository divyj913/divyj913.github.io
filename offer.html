Clarifications

Frequently asked
questions

Addressing concerns before we get to work. Open, honest expectations.

Who selects the clip highlights?
For the Growth and Authority retainers, I handle this completely. You upload the long-form file, and I scan it for high-value hooks, points, and stories. For the Starter tier, you provide the timestamps for the segments you want edited.
What is the onboarding process like?
We jump on a 30-minute kickoff call to align on your brand identity, color palettes, caption typography, and ideal client targets. Then, we set up a shared Google Drive/Frame.io folder. You drop files in, and the engine runs.
How does the guarantee work?
We agree on a delivery schedule (e.g. 5 clips every Monday). If a delivery is late by even an hour, you automatically get 5 additional clips added to your library for free. This holds me accountable to your posting consistency.
Are there other formats or packages available?
I specialize in high-ticket long-term relationships for the Founder Content Engine. However, if you require cinematic documentaries or long-form edits, we can discuss custom project rates during our strategy call.
